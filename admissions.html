Admissions

Moving in, made as gentle as possible.

We've designed our admissions journey with families who have been through it — five clear stages, at your pace, with real people at every turn.

01

Initial enquiry

Get in touch by phone, email or our contact form. We'll listen, understand what you're looking for, and answer your first questions with no pressure.

02

Assessment

A member of our care team visits to understand care needs, preferences and personal history — the small details that make care feel personal.

03

Tour & trial visit

Explore the home together, meet the team, and where possible arrange a short trial stay.

04

Personalised care plan

We build a plan around the resident's life, history and choices — reviewed with family before anything is finalised.

05

Moving in

Our team supports a warm, reassuring welcome, with a dedicated buddy for the first two weeks.
