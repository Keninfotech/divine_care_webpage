Our story

Care built on a quiet, uncompromising belief.

Divine Care was founded on a simple idea: that every person deserves care that is compassionate, dignified and personal. What began as a single home has grown into a trusted name across the UK, but our mission has never changed.

Our mission

To empower every resident to live with dignity, comfort and joy.

From a single home in 2001 to two considered homes in Scotland today, Divine Care has grown carefully, never faster than our ability to keep the promise we make to each new resident. Our teams are hand-picked, trained continuously, and led by clinicians who set the highest standards.
